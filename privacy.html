Privacy Policy & Disclaimer

Last updated: January 2025  ·  Effective immediately

Short version: BurnPlan runs entirely in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit your financial data anywhere. What you enter stays on your device.

Financial Disclaimer

BurnPlan is a free educational tool designed to help you model and explore retirement scenarios. It is not financial advice, and should not be relied upon as a substitute for guidance from a qualified financial advisor, tax professional, or investment planner.

All projections produced by BurnPlan are estimates based on the assumptions you provide. Future investment returns, inflation rates, tax laws, Social Security benefits, and market conditions are inherently uncertain and cannot be predicted with accuracy. Actual results will differ — possibly significantly — from any projection this tool produces.

Specifically, BurnPlan does not:

  • Guarantee any investment return or portfolio outcome
  • Account for all taxes, fees, or transaction costs
  • Provide personalized investment recommendations
  • Reflect current tax law changes or future legislative changes
  • Consider your complete financial picture, liabilities, or personal circumstances

We strongly recommend consulting a licensed financial advisor, CPA, or certified financial planner (CFP) before making any retirement, investment, or tax decisions. Use BurnPlan as a starting point for conversations — not as a final plan.

Social Security estimates are approximations only. For your actual Social Security benefit projections, visit ssa.gov/myaccount to create a my Social Security account and view your personalized statement.

RMD calculations use simplified IRS Uniform Lifetime Table divisors as a reference. Consult IRS Publication 590-B or a tax advisor for your exact required minimum distributions.

Privacy Policy

What data we collect

We collect no personal financial data. BurnPlan performs all calculations locally in your web browser using JavaScript. None of the numbers you enter — account balances, income, ages, or any other financial information — is ever transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Local storage

BurnPlan uses your browser's localStorage to save stress test results and scenario snapshots when you click "Save." This data is stored only on your device and is never sent anywhere. You can clear it at any time using the "Clear all" button in the Saved tab, or by clearing your browser's local storage in your browser settings.

Settings export files

When you use "Export settings," a JSON file is downloaded to your device. This file contains your plan configuration and is stored wherever you choose to save it on your computer. We have no access to this file.

Analytics & cookies

We may use standard web analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand how many people use BurnPlan and which features are most useful. This data is aggregated and anonymous — it does not include any financial information you enter into the tool.

Advertising

BurnPlan displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. For more information, see Google's advertising policies.

Advertisers do not have access to any information you enter into BurnPlan.

Third-party services

BurnPlan loads the following from third-party CDNs:

  • Chart.js — charting library from cdnjs.cloudflare.com
  • Google Fonts — DM Sans and DM Mono typefaces

These services may log your IP address as part of standard CDN operation. No financial data is shared with them.

Children's privacy

BurnPlan is intended for adults planning for retirement. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of BurnPlan after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
